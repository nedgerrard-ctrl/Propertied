Featured · May 2026

2026 Federal Budget: What it means for property investors.

The most significant shift in residential property investment taxation in a decade. New builds retain negative gearing. Established property does not. CGT treatment preserved for new-build investors.

The window to act closes 30 June 2027. PPM has prepared a plain-language briefing on what the changes mean, what is grandfathered, and what qualifies as a new build.

Read the Full Briefing

General information only. Announced policy, pending legislation.

Key takeaways at a glance

Negative gearing preserved for new builds from 1 July 2027

50% CGT discount election retained for new-build investors

Grandfathering: contracts before 7:30pm 12 May 2026

Window to qualify: contract before 30 June 2027

Off-the-plan apartments and townhouses qualify as new builds
