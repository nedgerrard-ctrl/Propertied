How we work

Every engagement starts
with a written brief.

Before we recommend anything, we listen. PPM's process begins with a detailed written brief — your objectives, timeline, capital position, risk tolerance and preferences. This is how consultants think. It is not how real estate agents typically behave.

Once we understand your brief, we work the market — not the other way around. You receive recommendations that match your criteria, with full disclosure of what we know and what we don't.

Zero buyer fees

Our acquisition advisory is paid by the developer or vendor. You receive independent guidance at no cost to you.

Portfolio capped at $100M

Our managed portfolio is deliberately limited so every client receives principal-level attention throughout the engagement.

One relationship, full lifecycle

Buy, manage, sell — all through the same senior partner. No handoffs. No junior staff handling your trust account.

For investors

The five-step investor journey.

A complete cycle — from sourcing to resale and reinvestment. Each step managed by PPM at principal level, with your interests as the only agenda.

01

We source

Only the best Melbourne projects enter our consideration. We apply the same development-side due diligence — feasibility, build quality, developer track record, location fundamentals — before recommending anything to a client.

02

You buy

Developer pays. The acquisition advisory fee is borne by the vendor. You receive independent guidance, contract review support, and a structured path from exchange to settlement — at zero cost to you.

Principal-level trust account oversight — always. Never a junior handling your rent, bond or disbursements. Our stewardship model is built on statutory accountability, not routine administration.

05

We replace

Capital moves into the next vetted property. The cycle begins again — informed by everything we have learned about your objectives across the previous engagement.

Property stewardship

Administration vs. management.
The distinction matters.

A property manager administers — they collect rent, arrange repairs, renew leases. These are necessary functions. But they are not the full extent of what your asset requires.

At PPM, management means understanding the trust account obligations attached to every dollar that passes through our hands. It means understanding the statutory consequences — VCAT, licence cancellation, criminal liability for breaches. It means treating your lease, your bond and your disbursements as though they were ours.

Your property is likely your most significant financial asset. We manage it as though it were ours.

Trust account oversight

All trust account obligations held at principal level. Rent, bond, disbursements — handled with statutory rigour, every time.

Maintenance that protects value

Led by George Tapia, our maintenance function operates to a standard that preserves asset value and prevents minor issues from becoming major liabilities.

No junior delegation

Your property is never handed to a junior property manager. Principal-level attention is a structural commitment, not a marketing promise.
