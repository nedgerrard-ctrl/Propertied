For Developers

Full-lifecycle partnership.
From feasibility to settlement.

PPM has been at the operational centre of Melbourne's development market since 2013. We know how projects succeed — and where they fail.

Developer partnership

We've worked both sides
of the transaction.

From 2018, PPM operated as a retained specialist in-house sales and strategy force for major Melbourne developers. We have lived the development process — not observed it from the outside. That inside knowledge is what makes us a different kind of project sales partner.

We understand what is at stake at each stage of a project. We know how pricing decisions are made, how launch sequencing affects absorption, and how finish selection influences both value and speed of sale. We bring that knowledge to every engagement.

As a selective third-party agent, we offer developers something that volume agencies cannot: direct access to a qualified, relationship-driven buyer database — and a team that presents projects with intelligence rather than pressure.

What we offer

Full development lifecycle
capability.

01

Feasibility advisory

Before a site is acquired, PPM can stress-test the project fundamentals — demand analysis, competitor landscape, target buyer profile and absorption velocity. Intelligence that informs the decision before capital is committed.

02

Finish selection

The gap between what buyers pay for and what developers assume buyers want is where value is lost. PPM's buyer-facing experience allows us to advise finish selection decisions with real market intelligence behind them.

03

Pricing strategy

Pricing that maximises revenue without stretching absorption. We apply the same analytical discipline used by the development teams we have worked alongside — not the gut-feel of a salesperson under quota pressure.

04

Launch sequencing

When you launch matters as much as what you launch. PPM advises on launch timing, sequencing and market positioning — drawing on real-time market intelligence that predates published data by months.

05

Sales management

For developers who want a specialist third-party sales force rather than an in-house team, PPM provides end-to-end sales management — from initial marketing to final contract exchange and settlement support.

06

Qualified buyer access

PPM's buyer database is built on relationships, not registrations. These are qualified individuals who have engaged seriously with us — ready to act when the right project is presented. No cold audiences, no unqualified lists.

Our selective approach

We only take on projects
we believe in.

PPM is selective. We do not take on every project that comes to us. We take on projects that we are prepared to present to our buyer clients — which means projects that meet our own quality standards for developer credibility, build quality and location fundamentals.

This selectivity is, paradoxically, what makes PPM valuable as a sales partner. Buyers who engage with PPM trust our recommendations. When we present a project, it has already been vetted. That trust translates to higher conversion rates and faster absorption than a developer could achieve through a volume agency presenting a undifferentiated slate of products.

If you are building something you believe in, we would like to hear about it.
