Our foundation

Not a standard
real estate agency.

PPM is not a standard real estate agency. We are professional management consultants who hold a real estate licence — a distinction that changes everything about how we work for you.

We apply consulting rigour and strategic discipline to a market that is often opaque, fast-moving and high-stakes. Our clients receive the kind of analysis and oversight that developers previously kept for themselves.

The business was built on one founding conviction: that the information advantage should sit with the buyer — not the seller.

Our journey

From the inside out.

2013

Founded selling boutique off-the-plan projects

PPM was established as a specialist sales agency for Melbourne's boutique new-development market. From day one, the model was selective — only the projects we believed in, presented honestly to buyers who deserved better information.

2018

Retained by major developers as specialist in-house force

As our track record grew, so did the mandates. PPM became the embedded specialist sales and strategy force for major Melbourne developers — operating with full visibility across feasibility, pricing, launch and settlement. This is the intelligence that now works for buyers.

2026

The insider knowledge becomes the buyers'

A strategic transition — from developer-facing specialist to buyer-facing partner. PPM now deploys all the same intelligence, relationships and processes on behalf of individual investors and owner-occupiers. Everything we learned from the other side of the table now works exclusively for you.

The PPM difference

Consulting discipline.
Real estate licence.

Most real estate agencies are run by salespeople. PPM is run by consultants who hold a licence. The difference is in how we think — analytically, strategically, with written briefs and rigorous due diligence applied to every property we recommend.

We don't earn from volume. We earn from relationships. Our portfolio is deliberately capped at $100M — so every client receives principal-level attention at every stage.
