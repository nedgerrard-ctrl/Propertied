I found out what the issue was when you tried to move the zipped file into your USB. It was to do with the format of the USB. By defaults, a lot of USBs pretty much have a maximum file size of 4GB due to the format it uses(FAT32). 
If you want to change it: 
1. Click on "This PC" on your file explorer
2. Right Click on the USB 
3. Click Format
4. Click the drop down bar under "File System"
5. Change it to  exFAT (FAT32 by default)

This will send you a warning saying all contents will be deleted, so make sure to back up any of the files you need in the USB before proceeding. 
After that you should be able to store files that are more than 6GB on the USB.
