2026 Federal Budget

The investment landscape has changed — in favour of new builds.

From 1 July 2027, new builds retain tax treatments that established housing loses. Negative gearing preserved. 50% CGT discount protected.

Off-the-plan apartments and townhouses are now one of the few remaining ways to invest in property with full tax effectiveness.

Read the Full Briefing

Negative gearing

Preserved for new builds. Restricted for established property purchased after 12 May 2026.

50% CGT discount

New build investors may elect to retain the existing discount. Other investors face an inflation-based model.

The window

Properties contracted before 7:30pm 12 May 2026 are grandfathered. Act before 30 June 2027 to lock in full new-build treatment.

General information only. Announced measures, pending legislation. PPM does not provide financial or taxation advice.
