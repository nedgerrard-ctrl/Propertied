---

## Visual tone

Premium but not ostentatious. Think high-end Melbourne residential developer brochure — restrained, confident, lots of white space, photography-led. The amber gold is used sparingly as an accent, not wallpapered across the design. The deep green grounds the palette. No bright colours, no gradients on UI elements, no playful or startup-style elements.

The site competes for the attention of overseas investors (primarily Asia-Pacific) and high-net-worth Melbourne owner-occupiers. Both audiences are sophisticated and will leave immediately if the design feels cheap or generic.

## Home page content structure (in order)

1. **Hero** — Full-screen image/video background. Headline: *New apartment and townhouse specialists in Melbourne.* Sub-line: *Business consultants. Licensed real estate agents. Off-the-plan specialists.* Two CTAs: Get in Touch / Learn More.

2. **Stats bar** — Three numbers: $1.5B+ Delivered · 1,000+ Settlements · $100M Portfolio cap

3. **What we do** — Two journeys side by side:
   - *For investors:* Complete end-to-end wealth-building. Source → Buy → Manage → Resell → Replace.
   - *For owner-occupiers:* Right home at the right price. We source, you buy (zero cost), we sell your existing home.

4. **Off-the-plan advantages** — Eight bullet points with brief explanations. Includes the 2026 Budget tax advantage.

5. **Who we are** — Business consultants. Licensed real estate agents. The distinction that changes everything. Not developers' sales arm — embedded in-house team.

6. **Why choose PPM** — Six cards (numbered 01–06): Insider knowledge · Real-time intelligence · Independent advice · One seamless relationship · Zero buyer fees · We manage your asset (not juniors)

7. **Our transition** — From industry insider to direct-to-market. 13 years → B2C via Monash partnership. No quotas, no developer agenda.

8. **2026 Federal Budget** — Negative gearing preserved for new builds. 50% CGT discount for new-build investors. The window is now.

9. **Testimonials teaser** — 3 short quotes with CTA to full testimonials page

10. **Footer CTA** — Start your property journey with PPM.

## What to avoid

- Generic real estate aesthetic (blue and white, stock photo couples holding keys)
- Over-designed hero animations or particle effects
- Bright or saturated colours
- Rounded, bubbly UI elements
- Marketing language ("passionate about property", "your dream home")
- Any reference to specific CRM, platform or third-party software names
- Heavy use of the amber — it is an accent colour only

---

## Reference feel

Architectural firm websites, high-end Melbourne off-the-plan developer brochures, private wealth management firms. Clean vertical rhythm, generous section spacing, photography doing the heavy lifting, type restrained and authoritative.
